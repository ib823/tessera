## VERIFIED CLAIMS

1. **Federal Constitution Article 121(1A) (inserted by Act A704 of 1988)** removes from civil courts any jurisdiction over matters within Syariah court jurisdiction. Source: AGC official Federal Constitution text.
2. **Federal Constitution Articles 11 and 12(4)** on religious freedom and minor's religious decision by parent/guardian — verified text.
3. **M. Indira Gandhi Federal Court ruling 2018 (Indira Gandhi Mutho v. Pengarah Jabatan Agama Islam Perak & Ors)** — landmark ruling that "parent" in Article 12(4) means both parents; civil courts can review syariah administrative decisions. Indira Gandhi's litigation indeed lasted ~11 years from 2009 to 2018.
4. **Loh Siew Hong case 2024:** Court of Appeal nullified three unilateral conversions; Perlis state law permitting unilateral conversion held unconstitutional (January 2024). Source: The Star 11 Jan 2024; International Christian Concern Nov 2024 follow-up.
5. **Perlis state government appealed 2023** to overturn the underlying Federal Court ruling. Source: DFAT Country Information Report Malaysia, 24 June 2024.
6. **Constitutional supremacy on conversion:** Both parents' consent required for minor conversion to be valid (multiple Federal Court decisions).
