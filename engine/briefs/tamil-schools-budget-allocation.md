## VERIFIED CLAIMS

1. **528 SJKT schools nationwide** (Tamil-medium primary). Source: Wikipedia compilation of MOE register (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lists_of_Tamil_national-type_primary_schools_in_Malaysia).

2. **Budget 2025 education sector: RM64.1 billion** (Bernama, StudyMalaysia) or **RM82.1 billion** by another framing (The Vibes). The discrepancy: RM64.1B is the operating allocation; the higher figure includes development. Either way the SJKT-specific line item is a small fraction.

3. **SJKT 2025 dedicated line item: RM50 million** (Varnam Malaysia citing announcement, benefits 28 SJKTs in Penang). Up from RM30 million previously. (https://varnam.my/113023/)

4. **SJKT 2024 split: RM6.09M for 90 government schools + RM19.48M for 277 government-aided** (Bernama/MOF, total ~RM25.6M line item).

5. **National school (SK) 2024 split: RM315.99M for 3,418 government schools + RM13.2M for 216 government-aided** (Bernama/MOF). Substantially higher per-school.

6. **Civil-society advocacy on Tamil school underfunding is well-documented.** Source: The Star "Help Tamil schools survive" March 2025 (https://www.thestar.com.my/news/education/2025/03/16/help-tamil-schools-survive).

## EDITORIAL FINDING

Same pattern as 1170 and 1581: real underlying issue (Tamil school underfunding documented in mainstream Malaysian media and by Indian-Malaysian civil society), unverifiable specifics in the cards. The "2.1% vs 5.4%" claim — the title of the issue — does not arithmetically reconcile with verified budget data.

### Recommendation: Hold (unpublish) and rewrite

A defensible rewrite would anchor on:
- Verified: 528 SJKT schools, RM50M dedicated 2025 line item, RM25.6M in 2024
- Verified comparator: SK RM315.99M for 3,418 schools (~RM92K/school) vs SJKT RM6.09M for 90 government schools (~RM68K/school) — a per-school gap, not the unsupported 5.4%/2.1% framing
- Verified phenomenon: chronic underfunding flagged by community advocates (The Star, Aliran)
- Drop: the 2.1%, 5.4%, 42% dilapidated, 22% teacher vacancy, 5.5% poverty specifics
